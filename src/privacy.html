USE OF COOKIES AND SIMILAR TECHNOLOGY

Tracking on our own App

As is commonly done on websites and applications, we may use cookies and similar technology to keep track of our users and the services they have elected. A "cookie" is a small text file containing alphanumeric characters that is stored on your computer or mobile device and uniquely identifies your browser. If you have created a user name and account, we will use cookies so that when you return to our App we can remember who you are and your preferences. We may allow business partners who provide services to our App to place cookies on your device that assist us in analyzing usage data. We do not allow these business partners to collect your personal information from our App except as may be necessary for the services that they provide.

You can manage these cookies. For example, you can configure your browser to accept all cookies, reject all cookies, or notify you when a cookie is set. If you disable cookies, it may interfere with the functionality of our App and you may not be able to use all of the App’s features.

We may also use web beacons or similar technology to help us track the effectiveness of our communications. A web beacon (also known as an action tag, a clear gif or pixel tag) is a1x1 pixel image embedded in a web page used to transparently collect information. For example, we may use a web beacon to count the number of visits to the various features of our App. If you have signed up to receive any of our e-mail newsletters, we use similar technology to count how many recipients have opened the message and clicked on one of its links.

Tracking by advertising services

Google Analytics use cookies and other, similar technologies, to collect information about how users use the App. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the App (including your IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States. Google may also transfer this information to third parties where required to do so by law, or where such third parties process the information on Google's behalf. Google will not associate your IP address with any other data held by Google. By using this App, you consent to the processing of data about you by Google in the manner and for the purposes set forth above. To learn more about Google Analytics, including how to opt out of it, visit https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

Third-party websites or services (i.e. Amazon and Facebook) may use cookies, pixels, and similar technology to collect or receive information upon using this App. We may share this information with the respective services or platforms, but we will not include your personal information.
